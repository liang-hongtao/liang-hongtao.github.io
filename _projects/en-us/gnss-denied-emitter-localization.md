**Program:** 2026 Postgraduate Research & Practice Innovation Program of Jiangsu Province

**Approval Date:** June 24, 2026

**Status:** Approved

As part of the research on embodied intelligence for electromagnetic spectrum management and control, this project studies how mobile intelligent agents can use wireless observations and active exploration to localize non-cooperative radio emitters in GNSS-denied environments.
